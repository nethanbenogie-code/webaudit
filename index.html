About This Platform

A complete SEO & visitor intelligence dashboard for website owners, marketers, and developers. All data stays private – stored locally or optionally synced to your own Google Sheet.

  • SEO Audit – Title, meta description, headings, alt text, viewport, HTTPS, robots, language, word count, DOCTYPE.
  • PageSpeed Insights – Performance, accessibility, best practices, SEO scores + Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS).
  • Visitor Map – See where your visitors come from on an interactive world map.
  • Time‑Series Charts – Daily, weekly, monthly visit trends.
  • Google Sheets Backup – Sync visitor data and user accounts to your own cloud sheet (optional).
  • Multi‑User & Roles – Admins can manage users, viewers can only view data.
  • Login History – Admins can see who logged in, when, and from which browser (consent required).
  • Export CSV – Download visitor history as a spreadsheet.
  • Customizable UI – Light/dark theme, accent colors.
🔐 Your data never leaves your browser unless you connect Google Sheets.
